Hallway Shooter

Introduction



Hallway Shooter is a revolutionary game decades ahead of its time. This game is a 3D game using the common program Scratch. It is designed to be a FPS or a First-Person Shooter in which you travel a series of hallways whilst shooting enemies know only as THE ORBS.






Gameplay



Hallway Shooter like any other FPS uses the keys WASD to navigate and the mouse to shoot. The actual gameplay is comprised of navigating the 4x4 square to find the markers and shooting the dreaded ORBS THAT KILLED YOUR FATHER. You also have a clip of 8 bullets for taking these enemys down.
